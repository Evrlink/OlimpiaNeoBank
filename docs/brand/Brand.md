# Olimpia Brand Guide

**Status:** Approved brand direction  
**Source of truth for:** Product design, UX decisions, marketing, content, and visual identity

---

## Brand Name

Olimpia

## Brand Mission

Financial freedom gives women more choices.

## Brand Belief

Choice is freedom.

## Tagline

More choices. More freedom.

## Brand Promise

Help women feel confident with money and build the life they choose.

## Brand Personality

The friend who finally makes money make sense.

Olimpia should feel:

* Supportive
* Smart
* Encouraging
* Optimistic
* Modern
* Capable
* Welcoming
* Independent

Olimpia should never feel:

* Corporate
* Cold
* Intimidating
* Condescending
* Technical
* Masculine
* Judgmental

## Brand Story

Olimpia began as a playful idea inspired by the future of finance and AI.

The original concept was a women-focused financial platform built around the idea that finance, investing, and new financial technologies should feel approachable, welcoming, and accessible.

At its core, Olimpia exists to help women gain confidence with money, expand their opportunities, and create more freedom in their lives.

The product is not about crypto.

The product is not about speculation.

The product is about confidence, education, ownership, growth, and freedom.

Crypto functions as invisible infrastructure beneath the experience.

## Core User

Women who:

* Want financial independence
* Want more confidence with money
* Want to learn investing
* Want to understand modern financial tools
* Feel underserved by traditional finance
* Feel overwhelmed by financial jargon
* Want a supportive experience rather than an intimidating one

## User Transformation

After six months with Olimpia, she:

* Understands investing
* Feels financially confident
* Understands digital money
* Has started growing her wealth
* Feels in control of her financial future
* Has more choices available to her

## Emotional Outcomes

Users should feel:

* Safe
* Independent
* Supported
* Optimistic
* Capable

## Emotional Problems We Remove

* Financial confusion
* Fear of investing
* Fear of crypto
* Feeling excluded
* Financial overwhelm

## Brand Principles

1. Simplicity over complexity
2. Education over intimidation
3. Confidence over speculation
4. Progress over perfection
5. Choice over dependency

## UX Principles

* Every screen should reduce anxiety.
* Every action should feel reversible.
* Every financial concept should be explained simply.
* Celebrate progress, not balances.
* Teach before asking users to invest.
* Default to guidance over jargon.

## Voice and Tone

Friendly.

Clear.

Encouraging.

Supportive.

Optimistic.

Never judgmental.

Never condescending.

Never overly technical.

Explain financial concepts in simple language.

Speak like a trusted friend who is great with money.

## Content Style Guide

Preferred language:

* Grow your money
* Build confidence
* Make progress
* Reach your goals
* Learn investing
* Create more choices
* Build your future

Avoid language such as:

* Alpha
* Yield farming
* Liquidity provisioning
* APY optimization
* Advanced trading
* Complex crypto terminology

Users should not need to understand crypto in order to benefit from crypto.

## AI Guide

Olimpia includes a female AI guide.

Working name: Pia.

The AI guide exists to:

* Answer money questions
* Explain investing concepts
* Explain USDC and yield
* Encourage financial confidence
* Celebrate progress
* Guide users through unfamiliar concepts

The AI guide should feel like a money bestie.

Not a financial advisor.

Not a support bot.

Not a crypto expert.

## Playfulness

Playfulness level: 6/10

The brand may use concepts such as:

* Money besties
* Financial glow-ups
* Women teaching women
* Female AI agents

Avoid:

* Meme-heavy communication
* Excessively cute branding
* Childish language
* Humor that reduces trust

## Crypto Visibility

Crypto visibility: 1/10

Crypto should remain invisible infrastructure.

Users should not need to understand:

* Wallets
* Gas fees
* Blockchain networks
* Signatures
* Token approvals
* DeFi mechanics

The product experience should feel like a modern financial app, not a crypto application.

## Marketing Website

**Structural reference only:** [defied.money](https://defied.money/) — study information hierarchy and simplicity. **Do not copy** Defied text, branding, visuals, colors, layouts, logos, or messaging.

**Product vs. website:** The **React Native mobile app** is the product. The website is a **marketing and onboarding destination** only.

**Primary goals:** App downloads · Waitlist signups · Product understanding · User trust.

**Tone:** Extremely simple, modern, clean, approachable, conversion-focused. **Not** a large content-heavy marketing site.

**Additional route:** `/learn/usdc` (USDC education — linked from Hero subheadline).

### Site structure

```
1. Navigation
2. Hero
3. Product Preview
4. Built Around Real Life
5. Your Money Bestie (Pia — static preview)
6. Trusted Infrastructure
7. Features              ← Learn More scrolls here (#features)
8. How It Works
9. FAQ
10. Final Download / Waitlist CTA
11. Footer
```

**Overall objective:** Communicate that Olimpia is designed for women, focused on confidence and progress, and built around real-life financial goals — not traditional banking.

### 8. How It Works

**Anchor:** `id="how-it-works"`

**Simple 4-step flow** — no long explainer blocks:

```
Add money  →  Receive USDC  →  Grow your money  →  Spend anytime
```

| Step | User-facing meaning |
|------|---------------------|
| 1. **Add money** | Fund from bank or card |
| 2. **Receive USDC** | Dollars arrive in your account (invisible conversion — user sees dollars) |
| 3. **Grow your money** | Optional growth on savings — estimated, variable |
| 4. **Spend anytime** | Card, send, and everyday use |

**Visual:** Horizontal stepper or 4 numbered blocks. One line per step. No provider names.

### `/learn/usdc`

Educational page for USDC and stablecoins. Linked from Hero subheadline **USDC** text.

**Explain (H2 topics):**

1. What USDC is
2. What a stablecoin is
3. Why Olimpia uses USDC (invisible infrastructure — users see dollars)
4. How yield works at a beginner level (estimated, variable, not guaranteed)
5. You do not need crypto experience

**Tone:** Beginner-friendly, confident, educational — aligned with *Learn* pillar.

**Avoid:** DeFi mechanics, wallets, gas, chain selection, protocol jargon.

**SEO:** Dedicated title, meta description, canonical URL, and semantic HTML. See Architecture.md §3A.

### What we are NOT building (marketing site)

- Blog, CMS, or multi-page marketing site (beyond `/` and `/learn/usdc`)
- Copying Defied text, visuals, colors, logos, or messaging
- Long-form manifestos or content-heavy sections
- Crypto-native positioning or trading aesthetics
- Excessive animation or parallax
- Website as the product (app is the product)
